Groundworks

Full groundworks packages, delivered as priced.

We work for main contractors and developers across Swindon and Wiltshire as a groundworks subcontractor — with our own plant, our own people and thirty years of doing exactly this.

Capability

What we deliver.

A complete groundworks scope, taken on as a package or in parts. If we price it, we can build it — and we say so before we price it, not after.

Bulk excavation & earthworks

Site strip, reduced level dig, cut and fill, muck-away and ground remediation — set out accurately and dug once.

Foundations & substructures

Strip, trench fill and raft foundations, reinforced concrete, ground beams and substructure brickwork to DPC.

Drainage

Foul and surface water drainage, attenuation and soakaways, connections and adoptable sewers, tested and recorded as we go.

Roads & sewers

Estate roads, kerbing and sub-base, Section 278 and Section 38 highway works, and Section 104 adoptable drainage.

Concrete works

Oversites, slabs, floors and hardstandings — poured, finished and cured properly, in sequence with the build programme.

External works & finishes

Driveways, footpaths, paving, boundary works and final grading — the part everyone sees, finished the way we'd want it on our own developments.

Plant & people

Our own kit. Our own operators.

We run our own fleet of excavators, dumpers and attachments, operated by people who work for us — not a different agency gang every fortnight.

That matters to a contracts manager for a simple reason: the crew that starts your job finishes it. Programmes hold because the same people are answerable for them, plant availability is ours to control, and standards on site don't depend on whoever the labour market sent that week.

Standards & assurance

The paperwork a contracts manager expects, without asking twice.

  • RAMS issued and agreed for every package before we start on site.
  • Carded workforce and ticketed plant operators, records available on request.
  • Insurances in place and evidenced at pre-start, not chased at valuation.
  • Drainage testing and records kept as the work proceeds, handed over complete.
  • References from main contractors and developers available on request.
How we work

Reliability isn't a value statement. It's a habit.

Priced straight.

Our tenders reflect what the job actually takes. We'd rather lose a package than win it on a number we can't build to — it's why the firms we work for come back.

Programmed honestly.

We commit to dates we can hold, flag problems while they're still cheap to fix, and keep the site tidy enough that the following trades can get on.

Send the drawings over, or just describe the job. We'll tell you quickly whether it's one for us.
